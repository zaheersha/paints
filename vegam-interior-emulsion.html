Vegam Interior Emulsion is engineered to deliver rich, consistent colour across all interior wall and ceiling surfaces. Its advanced acrylic binder ensures excellent adhesion to plaster, concrete and gypsum board surfaces, while the low-VOC formulation keeps indoor air quality safe from day one.

Application Steps

01

Surface Preparation

Ensure walls are clean, dry and free of dust, grease and loose particles. Fill cracks with Wallcare Putty and sand smooth once cured.

Allow putty to cure fully ‚Äî at least 24 hours ‚Äî before priming.
02

Apply Vegam Interior Primer

Apply 1‚Äì2 coats of Vegam Interior Primer to all surfaces. Allow each coat to dry fully before proceeding.

Primer coat is essential to ensure adhesion and uniform topcoat absorption.
03

First Coat

Dilute up to 10% with clean water. Apply evenly using a roller or brush. Work in sections to maintain a wet edge.

Apply in one direction first, then cross-roll to eliminate roller marks.
04

Second Coat

Allow the first coat to dry for a minimum of 4‚Äì6 hours. Apply second coat without dilution for maximum colour intensity and coverage.

Two coats are minimum for system warranty. A third coat may be needed for deep tones.
05

Clean Up

Clean tools with water immediately after use. Dispose of waste paint responsibly.
