About This Product

Vegam Interior Primer is formulated with a deep-penetrating acrylic resin that bonds firmly with plaster, concrete, brick and gypsum board surfaces. It consolidates friable surfaces and creates a uniform porosity for consistent topcoat absorption.

The alkali-resistant binder is critical on fresh cement plaster, where high alkalinity can cause saponification and peeling of standard emulsion topcoats. Vegam Interior Primer neutralises this effect, ensuring the topcoat adheres firmly throughout its service life.
