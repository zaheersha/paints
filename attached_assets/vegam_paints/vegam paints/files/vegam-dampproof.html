About This Product

Vegam Dampproof uses a high-build rubber-modified latex system that forms a tough, flexible waterproof film on the surface of the substrate. Unlike standard primers, it physically blocks water vapour and liquid water movement through the wall, addressing the root cause of damp patches rather than just covering them.

Use Vegam Dampproof as the first coat on any surface showing signs of moisture ingress, rising damp or water marks â€” before applying the full primer and topcoat system. Available for both interior and exterior applications.

01

Identify & Fix Source

Before applying Dampproof, identify and address the structural cause of moisture ingress where possible â€” roof leaks, plumbing leaks or failed waterproofing.

Dampproof will manage moisture transmission through walls but cannot replace structural waterproofing.
02

Surface Preparation

Remove loose paint, plaster and efflorescence. Clean the surface and allow to dry partially â€” surface should be damp but not wet for best adhesion.

03

Apply Dampproof

Apply undiluted using a stiff brush â€” work into cracks and pores. Apply in a scrubbing action for maximum penetration.

04

05
