Application Steps

01

Surface Preparation

Clean, dry surface. Allow new plaster 28 days. Remove loose paint.

02

Apply Cycle Interior Primer

Dilute up to 20% water. Apply by brush, roller or spray. Allow 4 hours before topcoat.

03

Apply Topcoat

Apply Cycle Interior Emulsion or Distemper within 24 hours of priming.
