Application Steps

01

Surface Preparation

Clean facade â€” remove dirt, efflorescence, algae and loose paint. Allow new plaster 28 days cure. Test moisture below 15%.

For efflorescence, treat with dilute acid wash, neutralise and allow to dry before priming.
02

Apply Exterior Primer

Dilute up to 10% with clean water. Apply using brush, roller or spray. Work primer into all pores and crevices.

03

Allow Full Cure

Allow primer to cure for minimum 6 hours before applying topcoat. Overnight cure recommended in humid conditions.

04

Apply Vegam Exterior Emulsion

Proceed with Vegam Exterior Emulsion as per its application guide.
