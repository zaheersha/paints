About This Product

Cycle Distemper is a dry powder distemper that delivers a clean, flat matte finish on interior walls and ceilings. Mixed on-site with water, it provides a cost-effective interior coating for projects where the primary objective is clean, uniform coverage at the lowest practical cost.

Ideal for rental properties, low-cost housing projects, contractor mass-painting work and any interior space where budget is the primary constraint. Available in white and a range of standard pastel tones.

Add distemper powder gradually to clean water in a bucket â€” 1.5 parts water to 1 part powder by volume. Stir to a smooth, lump-free consistency. Allow to soak 10 minutes and restir.

02

Apply 1 coat of Cycle Interior Primer on fresh plaster before distemper. Allow to dry.

03

Apply using a distemper brush in long, even strokes. Work in one direction across the wall.
