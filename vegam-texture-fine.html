About This Product

Vegam Texture Fine uses a fine polymer aggregate to create a subtle, sophisticated textured surface that adds tactile depth without the boldness of a rustic finish. The resulting surface catches light gently, giving interiors a premium quality that smooth emulsion cannot replicate.

Particularly popular with interior designers specifying for luxury residential projects, premium hospitality and high-end retail. Available in the full Vegam colour range ‚Äî the fine texture beautifully enhances the depth of rich or muted tones.

Application Steps

01

Surface Preparation

Clean, dry, sound surface. Apply Vegam Interior Primer and allow 6‚Äì8 hours cure.

02

Apply with Float or Sponge Roller

Apply with a plastic float in thin, even 1‚Äì2mm layers. Alternatively, a sponge roller creates an even fine-textured pattern automatically.

Sponge roller method is faster and more consistent for large areas.
03

Create Texture

For float application: while wet, create the fine texture using the flat face of the float in circular or vertical sweeping motions.

04

Allow Cure & Second Coat

Allow 24 hours before second coat if additional depth is required. Second coat significantly deepens the textural effect.
