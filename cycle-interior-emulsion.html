About This Product

Cycle Interior Emulsion is a water-based acrylic emulsion that delivers a smooth, washable matte finish on interior walls and ceilings. While it does not carry the advanced performance features of Vegam Interior Emulsion, it provides reliable coverage, adequate washability and satisfactory colour retention at a significantly lower cost.

The ideal choice for large-volume builder projects, rental properties and any interior space where good-quality finish is needed but premium specification is not required.

Application Steps

01

Surface Preparation

Ensure walls are clean, dry and sound. Fill cracks. Allow new plaster to cure 28 days.

02

Apply Cycle Interior Primer

Apply 1 coat Cycle Interior Primer or Cycle Multi Primer. Allow 4 hours cure.

03

First Coat

Dilute up to 15% water. Apply with roller or brush. Allow 4 hours before second coat.

04

Second Coat

Apply second coat at full consistency for maximum coverage and colour depth.
